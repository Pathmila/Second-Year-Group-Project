Best Places In Sri Lanka


Ella

Ella is a small village in Sri Lanka's highlands, packed with tea estates, mountains, waterfalls and some good air to breathe, of course. Spectacular sights to see and catch are views of mist and clouds covering the summits of the mountain range and the morning sun rising in Ella.After a fresh morning hike, reach a waterfall, catch some delicious Sri Lankan food with a beer from somewhere, rest for a while and go for a railwalk to the famous Nine Arch Bridge at night, you'd certainly love the whole experience.

Arugambay

Arugam Bay is located on the South East Coast of Sri Lanka, in the dry zone of the Indian Ocean. The bay is 320 KM to the east of Colombo. Arugam Bay is regarded as one of the world's best spots for surfing. One of Sri Lanka's best known and most beloved beaches is Panama Beach. Panama Beach is very clean, blackish gray, sandy beaches, like scuba diving and fishing, to engage in any possible water sport.

Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa, in southwestern Sri Lanka, is a seaside resort town. It is renowned for its good surf and beaches lined with restaurants and bars, including palm-dotted Hikkaduwa Beach. Hikkaduwa National Park, which is a reef sanctuary and home to sea turtles and exotic fish, is sheltered by the shallow waters opposite Hikkaduwa Beach. Gangarama Maha Vihara, inside is a Buddhist temple adorned with murals drawn by hand.

Kithulgala

The town is called Kitulgala, where wildlife lives, plants grow, with no inhibitions at all, fish and water creatures strut about. Except for some shallow sections on the opposite shore, the Kellani River is healthy and it is a very secluded place to enjoy swimming and water sports such as rafting. People love lake side picnics and are often seen indulging in their favorite water sports. Wild and harmless fish swim around the lake; it offers a natural get-away for people who find peace in everything.
